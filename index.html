Yellow Bingo PIES

Yellow Bingo PIES Rules

  1. You can mark a square when you hear a call of "Yellow Tango PIES" for a station from SkyTrain Control.
    • "Yellow Tango GIMS" do not count
    • "Red Tango" calls from the platform do not count
    • A "Yellow Tango PIES" call from Control that turns out to be a "Red Tango" is permitted
  2. In the event of a "Red Sierra", the game is suspended.
  3. A board may only be used for a continuous session. You may not "pause" a game and resume it at a later time.
  4. The game is suspended at the end of revenue service.
